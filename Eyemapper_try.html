Eyemapper is a product that aims to help visually impaired people detect nearby obstacles. Our product uses a technique called stereo imaging to create a virtual map in order to prevent the visually impaired ones from bumping into nearby objects. We started Eyemapper because my grandfather suffers from Macular degeneration which hinders him from walking outside in the street. This idea came into my mind and pushed me to try to make some changes, possibly benefiting others like my grandfather in the society.
